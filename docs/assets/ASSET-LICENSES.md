## APPROVED ASSET SOURCES (for Stage 3)

### Option A: Tarot Deck (Recommended)
**Rider-Waite Tarot (1909)** — Public Domain in most jurisdictions
- Original art by Pamela Colman Smith, published 1909
- Copyright expired (>120 years old)
- Source: Wikimedia Commons
- URL: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Rider-Waite_tarot_deck
- **Verification needed:** Some newer colorizations/scans may have new copyright
- **Action:** Use scans from pre-1928 publications only (US public domain)
- **Alternative:** Use openly licensed recreations

**Universal Waite Tarot** — NOT public domain (U.S. Games Systems owns rights)
- ❌ Do NOT use without license from U.S. Games Systems

### Option B: Custom/CC0 Deck
- Search: https://openclipart.org (search "tarot")
- Search: https://unsplash.com (search "tarot card")
- Create custom minimalist line-art (recommended for brand consistency)
- Commission artist with work-for-hire agreement

### Option C: License a Deck
- U.S. Games Systems: commercial licensing available (contact directly)
- Expected cost: $500-2000 one-time for digital rights
